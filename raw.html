    • Camera Raw Files

    Digital cameras produce image data called camera raw files. The exact data recorded by the camera's sensor is preserved in an uncompressed, raw state. There are numerous varieties of camera raw formats since every type of digital camera has a unique sensor. A application must support both the file type and the particular camera model that took the image in order to open a camera raw file. The.DNG,.CR2,.NEF, and.ARW file extensions are frequently used for camera raw files.
